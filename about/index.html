About

This website provides country-level data, rankings, and comparisons for economic, demographic, geographic, environmental, trade, and public finance indicators.

The goal is to make international statistics easier to explore by presenting key indicators in a simple, searchable, and comparable format.

Users can compare countries and territories across indicators such as GDP, GDP per capita, GDP growth, inflation, population, life expectancy, area, trade, public finance, and reserves.

Linking to This Website

You may link to any page on this website, including the homepage, country pages, charts, and ranking pages. No prior permission or contact is required.

When sharing data or charts, please link to the relevant page so users can check the source and latest values.

Estimates and Projections

Some indicators may include estimates or projections depending on the source, country, territory, and year. Please refer to the original source for detailed methodology.

Primary and Supplementary Sources

Some indicators are primarily based on one data source, but supplementary sources may be used for specific countries, territories, or years to improve coverage. When supplementary sources are used, source details are noted on the relevant indicator pages.

Regional Classification

Regional groupings on this website are based on the UN M49 standard. For geographic browsing, Central America and the Caribbean are included under North America.

Country and Territory Coverage

This website covers countries and selected territories listed in the UN M49 "countries or areas" standard. For easier browsing, entries are shown as either countries or territories on this website. This distinction is used for navigation and display purposes and does not imply any position on sovereignty or political status.

Flag Icons

Flag icons are used for visual identification. Some territories are shown with the flag of the administering country, while others may be shown with local or territorial flags depending on official status and common local usage. These icons do not imply any position on sovereignty, recognition, or political status.

Official languages

Official languages are listed based on official status and commonly recognized national-level usage.

Rounding of Figures

Displayed figures may be rounded for readability. Rankings may be calculated using the underlying source data rather than the rounded values shown on the page.

Euro Area Local Currency Data

For countries that have adopted the euro, local-currency figures for years before euro adoption may be shown in euros, converted from former national currencies using official fixed conversion rates. For details, please refer to the IMF World Economic Outlook FAQ.
