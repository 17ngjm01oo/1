About

This website provides country-level data, rankings, and comparisons for economic, demographic, geographic, environmental, trade, and public finance indicators.

The goal is to make international statistics easier to explore by presenting key indicators in a simple, searchable, and comparable format.

Users can compare countries and territories across indicators such as GDP, GDP per capita, GDP growth, inflation, population, life expectancy, area, trade, public finance, and reserves.

Linking to This Website

You may link to any page on this website, including the homepage, country pages, charts, and ranking pages. No prior permission or contact is required.

When sharing data or charts, please link to the relevant page so users can check the source and latest values.

Official languages

Official languages are listed based on official status and commonly recognized national-level usage.
